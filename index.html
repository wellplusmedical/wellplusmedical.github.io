Chronic Care Management (CCM) for Medicare Patients

Comprehensive care coordination for Medicare patients with 2+ chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, COPD, and arthritis.

  • 24/7 access to our care team for urgent questions
  • Monthly check-ins between visits to keep you on track
  • Personalized care plan shared across your providers
  • Medication & appointment coordination to help avoid ER visits

Medicare covers CCM; standard coinsurance may apply depending on your supplemental coverage.

Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) Services

Advanced health monitoring with connected devices (blood pressure monitors, glucose meters, digital scales) that transmit readings directly to our medical team for proactive care.

  • Simple devices, easy setup
  • Daily / near-real-time review by our team
  • Early interventions to prevent complications
  • Works great alongside CCM

Medicare coverage available; device/monitoring cost-share may vary.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept Medicare and other insurance plans?

Yes, we accept Medicare and most major insurance plans including United Healthcare, Blue Cross Blue Shield (BCBS), Humana, Optimum Healthcare, Freedom Health, Devoted Health, Florida Blue, Aetna, Cigna, Ambetter, Multiplan, and Tricare. Please call our office to verify your specific plan coverage.

Do you offer same-day appointments and walk-in services?

Yes! We welcome walk-in patients and offer same-day appointments at our Zephyrhills, Lakeland, and Lutz locations. We also provide 24/7 on-call support for all locations so help is available after hours. No appointment is required for walk-in visits, though scheduling ahead is recommended for guaranteed availability.

What services do you provide at your family practice?

We provide comprehensive primary care including annual physicals, chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, COPD, heart disease), weight management, women's health services, joint injections, dermatologic procedures, acute care for infections and injuries, telehealth visits, and Medicare Annual Wellness Visits. We also offer Chronic Care Management (CCM) and Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) programs for eligible Medicare patients.

Where are your clinic locations?

We have three convenient locations. Our Zephyrhills clinic is at 6151 Fort King Rd, Zephyrhills, FL 33542 (phone 813-782-5518). Our Lakeland clinic is at 1230 W. Daughtery Rd, Lakeland, FL 33810 (phone 863-858-3884). Our Lutz clinic is at 17863 Hunting Bow Cir, Suite 101, Lutz, FL 33558 and is reachable at our Zephyrhills line, 813-782-5518. Our locations serve patients throughout Pasco County, Polk County, Hillsborough County, and the greater Tampa Bay area in Florida.

What are your clinic hours?

Our Zephyrhills and Lakeland clinics are open in person Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Our Lutz clinic at 17863 Hunting Bow Cir, Suite 101 is open Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. All three locations offer 24/7 on-call support for after-hours needs.

Are your doctors board certified?

Yes, both Dr. Parth Patel and Dr. Omprakash Pansara are Board Certified in Family Medicine. Dr. Patel is also a Diplomate of the American Board of Obesity Medicine, providing specialized expertise in weight management and obesity treatment.

How can I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment online through our secure booking system, call our Zephyrhills or Lutz office at (813) 782-5518 or our Lakeland office at (863) 858-3884, or simply walk in during business hours. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your needs.
